Introducing Wavebox

WMail is changing its name to Wavebox, whilst adding support for far more than just e-mail. Google Inbox, Gmail, Outlook, Office 365, Slack, Trello & any Web link right at your fingertips

That's not all though. Wavebox is jam-packed with more features! Here's a brief overview of some of the other new things that that come in Wavebox...

  • Better Google Services integration.
  • Integrations with Microsoft Services.
  • Google Docs & Office editing.
  • Sleeping tabs to save you oodles of memory more.
  • Services toolbar.
  • Supercharged Google Notifications.
  • Automatic updates on macOS & Windows.
  • Signed Binaries for easier install & added security.
  • Still open-source on Github.
